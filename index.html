Introducing: Hallow-Cat | The greatest motivational companion!

Meet 'Hallow-Cat'; A little cat-bot created for professional procrastinators. Hallow-Cat helps you with waking up, as well as helping you out with your procrastination problems. Set alarms to either wake up or use for deadlines, or put your phone in the handy phone-slot to keep your phone safely locked away so you genuinely focus on your work. Hallow-Cat is fully customizable in terms of setting time. Whenever you want to wake up, or just set an alarm in general; she's your friend. It's extremely easy!

- Features -

Professional Alarm

Have trouble waking up? Put your phone on snooze too often when it rings? Hallow-Cat has got your back. It's simple; set the alarm, and when it goes off, she will make it her duty to whine till you get up and pick her up to take off her alarm.

Phone Thief

Next to being a fantastic alarm, Hallow-Cat also stores your phone safely in its phone-slot for whenever you're procrastinating. When sliding the phone into the phone-slot, she runs away with it. When you're done working, you can take it back out.

Customizable

Hallow-Cat is fully customizable in terms of setting time. Whenever you wanna wake up, or just set an alarm in general; she's your friend. It's extremely easy! Need to wake up for school or work, Hallow-Cat's got you covered. Setting a deadline? Done.

- How -

How we developed Hallow-Cat is very simple. First we interview our target audience to see what issues they're having and then from there, we develop our product. Hallow-Cat was then created from a series of concept-art, using Circuit Playgrounds, various other bibs and bobs, and of course; the code powering the machine.

- Why -

The reason why we developed Hallow-Cat is not only because we had a school project surrounding the subject-matter, but also the drive to create something that actually helps someone. Through careful consideration and planning, this is our end product!
